What is Urban Heat Island Effect?

Are higher temperatures in developed cities. This happens because man made materials are efficient in absorbing heat during the day and release it at night. Buildings in general are like pockets of heat, or islands of heat releasing the heat at night.

How do we contribute?

We can increase the effect by planting more concrete, making more pavement, making more and tall building constraining the space within preventing the air circulating in the city.

URBAN DESIGN + NATURE = COOLER PLACES

What is sustainable urban design?

Is an environmental approach in designing urban spaces for human activity with the concept of implementing more nature and minimizing the building’s footprint.

It tries to combine nature with the artificial landscaping of the man-made cities to cool it off, to make it more habitable and sustainable.
